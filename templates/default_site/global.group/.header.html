Our Vision

Population-Based Health Systems

Our vision is to assist communities throughout the United States in developing a population-based health system that reflects the health needs and preferences of the segments of our community, with service and access solutions that are appropriate in scale and cost, developed through collaboration and cooperation between the communities and the health sector.
