Randall Lewis Health Policy Fellowship program

The Randall Lewis Health Policy Fellowship program is now seeking applications for the 2012-2013 academic year. This prestigious and competitive fellowship, the first of its kind in the Inland Empire, will provide a unique opportunity to work directly with local communities in improving residents' health. Fellows will develop the skills required to successfully influence local and regional health policy.

Applications are due no later than July 30, 2013.
